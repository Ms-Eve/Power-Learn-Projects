Menu

Indulge in a culinary journey with our diverse and tempting menu at our restaurant. From hearty appetizers to decadent desserts, our menu offers a wide range of dishes crafted with the finest ingredients and a mix of traditional and modern flavors. Whether you're craving a savory pasta, a juicy steak, or a fresh seafood dish, our menu has something for every palate. Join us for a dining experience that will delight your taste buds and leave you craving for more.

Appetizers



  • Crispy Calamari- Tender strips of calamari lightly breaded and fried to a golden crisp, served with a zesty marinara sauce for dipping.
  • Caprese Salad - A refreshing salad featuring ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella cheese, fragrant basil leaves, and drizzled with balsamic glaze and olive oil.
  • Loaded Potato Skins - Crispy potato halves filled with melted cheddar cheese, crispy bacon bits, and topped with green onions and sour cream for a decadent and satisfying starter.

Main Courses



  • Grilled Ribeye Steak - A mouthwatering ribeye steak, seasoned to perfection and grilled to your desired level of doneness, served with garlic mashed potatoes and seasonal vegetables.
  • Seafood Linguine- A delectable medley of shrimp, mussels, and scallops tossed in a savory garlic white wine sauce with linguine pasta, garnished with fresh herbs and Parmesan cheese.
  • Stuffed Chicken Breast - Tender chicken breast stuffed with spinach, sun-dried tomatoes, and creamy goat cheese, baked to a golden brown and served with wild rice pilaf and a side of roasted vegetables.

Desserts



  • Classic New York Cheesecake- A rich and creamy cheesecake with a buttery graham cracker crust, topped with a sweet raspberry coulis and whipped cream.
  • Molten Lava Cake - A decadent chocolate lava cake with a gooey center that oozes out when cut into, served warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream and a drizzle of chocolate sauce.
  • Creme Brulee- A creamy custard dessert topped with a caramelized sugar crust, made with Madagascar vanilla beans for a rich and aromatic flavor profile.
