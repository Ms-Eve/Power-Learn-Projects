About Me

INFORMATION ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Evelyn, and I am a junior front-end developer with
experience in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I have a passion for creating
visuallyappealing websites and user interfaces that are both functional and
user-friendly.

I have worked on a variety of projects, including creating responsive websites,
implementing interactive features using JavaScript frameworks such as jQuery and React

In addition to my technical skills, I have a keen eye for design and a strong attention to
detail. I am constantly learning and refining my skills to stay up-to-date with the latest
trends and technologies in front-end development.

I am excited to showcase my work and skills on my online portfolio, where you can view some of
my recent projects and learn more about me and my journey as a front-end developer. I am always
open to new opportunities and collaborations, so feel free to reach out to me with any inquiries or
feedback. Thank you for visiting my portfolio!
